Akasha · Bridge Burners

a design system · briefing decks · component kits · David Lee Wise / ROOT0
Akasha is a design system built for one purpose: to hold AI-ethics work with the seriousness it deserves — human–AI rights documents, accountability ledgers, briefing decks, and the quiet sites that carry them. It is a complete visual language — colour, type, editorial marks, badges, buttons, cards, forms — under the codename Bridge Burners. The pieces below are all live; the component gallery shows every part of the kit.
